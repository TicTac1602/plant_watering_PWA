About Water Me

Welcome to Water Me, your personal plant watering assistant!

Water Me helps you keep track of your plant's watering schedules so that you can ensure they receive the care they need to thrive.

Features:

  • Track watering schedules for each plant
  • Receive notifications for upcoming watering tasks
  • Add new plants with custom watering frequencies
  • View and manage your plant collection easily

Stay organized and keep your plants healthy with Water Me!
